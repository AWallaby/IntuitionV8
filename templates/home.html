Our Mission

In pre-university studies, students often find it daunting to create portfolios to aid in their university or future job applications.

We want to ease this process and help students find the best activities that are most suited to their passion, and by shortlisting the best activities that is most worth their time and effort.

Ultimately, we hope to create a plotform for seinors to share their experiences, and recommend valuable opportunities to newer batches.
